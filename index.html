Being a sole trader in the health and fitness industry is hard enough without all of the overheads associated with administration.

Health Me aims to help take the headaches out of running a small business by creating a beautiful and simple to use Application.

There are other applications in this space, but we aim to create a range of applications (web and mobile) that are a delight to use, perfect for the busy professional.

Features

  • Scheduling Manage schedules and publish timetables
  • Customizeable Present timetables with pleasant and easy to use themes
  • Accept Payments Allow clients to make direct payment for bookings with a simple clean interface.
  • Reminders Notify clients and Traders of upcoming classes
  • Social Hooks Easily publish class/session information to social media such as facebook/twitter/linkedin etc
  • Invoicing/Accounting Send out customised invoices and more.
